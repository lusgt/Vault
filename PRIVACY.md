# Privacy / 隐私说明

**中文** | **English**

---

## 数据收集 / Data Collection
Vault 不收集任何用户个人数据。

Vault does not collect any personal data.

---

## 数据存储 / Data Storage
所有数据仅保存在本地，并经过加密处理。

All data is stored locally and encrypted.

---

## 网络访问 / Network Access
应用不依赖云端服务，默认不上传任何数据。

The app does not rely on cloud services and does not upload any data by default.

---

## 第三方服务 / Third-Party Services
默认不集成第三方分析、日志或广告服务。

No third-party analytics, logging, or advertising services are integrated by default.
